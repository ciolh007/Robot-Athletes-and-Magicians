Moravec's Paradox

  • Assumption: Higher level (symbolic/conscious) reasoning is most important part of AI
    • Play chess, Go, ...
  • Truth: Hardest part is subconscious reasoning
    • Get up
  • Current state of AI (ML) uses deep learning. Scale is all you need

Artificial Intelligence

  • Robustness and versatility are important aspects of intelligence
  • Humans are bad at solving problems optimally
  • Our robots can outperform humans is very specific tasks (machine vision, pick and place)
  • But they also walk into a wall three times in a row
  • Minimum time of fully autonomous operation (Don't Mess Up Period)
  • Paper and videos are patient
  • Robot competition provide crucial benchmarks for guiding research
  • Combining sports and robotics is a natural way to motivate teams and spectators
  • Federation of International Robot Sports Association (FIRA) http://www.firaworldcup.org presents several leagues for robot athletes
  • In 2019, we had approximately 1,200 participants at FIRA Robot WorldCup in Changwon, Korea
  • In 2022, we organize the FIRA 2022 SimulCup. Team compete from different local hubs
